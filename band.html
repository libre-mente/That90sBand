Meet the Band

Rafe, Monte, Matt, Melinda, and Ashlee—built for tight performances and crowd-first energy.

About That 90’s Band

That 90’s Band is a 90s cover band focused on delivering high-energy performances that keep the dance floor moving. From guitar-driven anthems to classic keyboard textures, we bring a full, polished live experience to venues across Colorado and northern New Mexico.

Rafe — Guitar

Rafe brings the punch: clean tone, precise leads, and rhythm that locks in with the drummer. His playing is built to capture the feel of the original 90s tracks—right down to the details.

Lead + Rhythm Tone Focus

Monte — Bass

Monte drives the groove with steady low-end momentum. From funky pockets to driving rock lines, he keeps everything locked and gives the band that “can’t-sit-still” energy.

Groove Big Low End

Matt — Keyboards

Matt adds the classic 90s texture—synth lines, atmospheric fills, and the hooks that make songs instantly recognizable. The sound is fuller, warmer, and undeniably “that era.”

Synth Texture Stage Energy

Melinda — Drums

Melinda anchors the show with tight timing and dynamic control. She keeps the band crisp, fills the space with style, and powers the transitions that keep the crowd engaged start to finish.

Precision Power + Feel

Ashlee — Singer

Ashlee delivers the vocals that bring the songs to life—confident delivery, memorable tone, and crowd-leading energy. Expect big sing-alongs and a performance that feels like the soundtrack.
