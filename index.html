Meet the crew

Each member gets one funny 90s-flavored bio. Because brevity is totally radical.

We’re a full-stack 90s cover experience: guitars that crunch like a dial-up modem, bass lines that make your neighbors question their life choices, keys that glow like a CRT in the dark, drums tight enough to soundtrack a skateboard montage, and vocals that would absolutely survive a “World’s Best Karaoke Night.”

Booking us is basically the easiest way to time-travel to the era of tamagotchis, blockbuster posters, and “one more song” turning into “wow, it’s midnight again.”
