Band bio (aka why it’s a good idea to come)

That 90’s Band brings the kind of throwback fun that makes you remember when “buffering” was something your parent said about soup, not the internet. We cover the classics with modern-tight confidence, so whether you came for the guitar hero moments, the bass-face grooves, or the singalong that turns strangers into friends—your night’s about to get very, very nostalgic.

Expect high-energy sets, punchy vibes, and the occasional “Did we just reference that?” wink—because the 90’s never really ended, it just changed outfits.
